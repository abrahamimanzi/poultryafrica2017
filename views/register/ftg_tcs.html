Eligibility criteria

  1. Do you have a registered company operating in Africa.
  2. Are you seeking to raise above $25,000 of capital (debt or equity) within the next 6 to 12 months.
  3. Are you an experienced entrepreneur and a strong management team with relevant expertise.
  4. Can you demonstrate strong economic, social and/or environmental impact through the business model success
  5. Do you have a prototype and/or proven concept and can demonstrate traction
  6. Are you generating revenues underpinned by contracts or established relationships
  7. Do you offer a scalable market opportunity and a source of sustainable competitive advantage
  8. Can you provide a robust business plan with a set of credible financial projections.
  9. Are you coming from a Member State of Smart Africa? See member states here.

Please do not apply if you are:

  1. A local authority, sole trader or unincorporated entity
  2. Looking to raise investment to purely fund research and development or proof of concept activities
  3. Looking for purely grant funding.
